- ▢ 1 banana, chopped
- ▢ 1 cup rava
- ▢ ¾ cup poha / avalakki
- ▢ 1 cup curd
- ▢ 2 tbsp jaggery
- ▢ ½ tsp cardamom powder
- ▢ ½ tsp salt
- ▢ ¼ tsp turmeric
- ▢ ¼ tsp baking soda
- Firstly, in a mixer jar take 1 banana.
- Add 1 cup rava, ¾ cup poha, 1 cup curd, 2 tbsp jaggery, ½ tsp cardamom powder and ½ tsp salt.
- Blend to smooth batter adding water as required.
- Transfer the batter into the large bowl and add ¼ tsp turmeric.
- Mix well making sure everything is well combined.
- Also, add ¼ tsp baking soda and mix gently forming a frothy batter.
- Pour the batter over the hot tawa.
- Cook until the top of the dosa is cooked completely.
- Finally, enjoy Instant Banana Dosa with chutney or honey.

- Firstly, instead of rava you can use wheat flour for a smooth texture.
- Also, you can add eno fruit salt in place of baking soda.
- Additionally, adding jaggery gives sweetness. So adjust the amount of jaggery depending on the sweetness of the banana.
- Finally, Instant Banana Dosa Recipe tastes great when made soft and spongy.
