- ▢ 1½ cup besan / gram flour
- ▢ ¼ cup rava / suji
- ▢ ¼ cup curd
- ▢ ¼ tsp turmeric
- ▢ ¾ tsp salt
- ▢ 1 cup water
- ▢ oil, for roasting
- ▢ 1 cup paneer, grated
- ▢ 1 carrot, chopped
- ▢ ½ capsicum, chopped
- ▢ 1 tomato, chopped
- ▢ 3 tbsp sweet corn
- ▢ 2 tbsp coriander, chopped
- ▢ ½ tsp salt
- firstly, in a mixer jar take 1½ cup besan, ¼ cup rava, ¼ cup curd, ¼ tsp turmeric and ¾ tsp salt.
- add 1 cup water and blend to smooth batter.
- pour the batter into a large bowl and rest for 10 minutes.
- meanwhile, to prepare the stuffing, in a bowl take 1 cup paneer, 1 carrot, ½ capsicum, 1 tomato, 3 tbsp sweet corn, and 2 tbsp coriander.
- add ½ tsp salt and mix well.
- paneer stuffing is ready.
- to prepare the chilla, heat the dosa pan and pour the chilla batter.
- spread as thin as possible.
- once the chilla is cooked from the top, spread 1 tsp oil.
- cover and roast on low flame until the chilla is cooked well.
- now spread prepared paneer stuffing uniformly and cook for some time.
- roll the chilla tight keeping the stuffing intact.
- finally, enjoy Besan Chilla Recipe with green chutney and tomato sauce.

- Additionally, adding rava to the chilla batter gives nice crispiness to the cheela.
- Finally, Besan Chilla Recipe tastes great when served hot and spicy.
