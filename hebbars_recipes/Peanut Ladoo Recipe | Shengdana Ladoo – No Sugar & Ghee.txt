- ▢ ½ kg peanut
- ▢ ½ kg jaggery
- ▢ 2 tbsp cashew, chopped
- ▢ 2 tbsp pumpkin seeds
- ▢ 2 tbsp sunflower seeds
- ▢ 2 tbsp walnut, chopped
- ▢ 2 tbsp poppy seeds
- ▢ ½ tsp cardamom powder
- Firstly, in a kadai take ½ kg of peanut and roast on low flame.
- Roast until the peanuts turn crunchy and the skin separates.
- Cool completely and remove the skin from peanuts.
- Pulse and grind to a coarse texture.
- Transfer the peanuts to the large plate and keep aside.
- Also, take a few roasted peanuts into the mixer jar along with ½ kg jaggery.
- Pulse and grind to a coarse texture.
- Transfer the mixture to the same plate and mix well.
- Roast, 2 tbsp cashew, 2 tbsp pumpkin seeds, 2 tbsp sunflower seeds, 2 tbsp walnut and 2 tbsp poppy seeds.
- Dry roast till the nuts turn crunchy.
- Transfer the roasted nuts and ½ tsp cardamom powder. Mix well.
- Make sure to crumble and mix the mixture turns moist and well combined.
- Start preparing the ladoo.
- Finally, enjoy Peanut Ladoo Recipe for a month when stored in airtight container.
