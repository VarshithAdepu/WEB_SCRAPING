- ▢ 2 tomato, cubed
- ▢ 1 cup rice flour
- ▢ ½ cup rava / suji
- ▢ ½ tsp chilli powder
- ▢ 1 tsp salt
- ▢ water, for batter
- ▢ 2 tbsp coriander, finely chopped
- ▢ few curry leaves, chopped
- ▢ 1 inch ginger, grated
- ▢ 1 tsp cumin
- ▢ oil, for roasting
- Firstly, in a mixer jar take 2 tomato, 1 cup rice flour, ½ cup rava, ½ tsp chilli powder and 1 tsp salt.
- Grind to smooth batter adding 2 cup water.
- Transfer the batter to a large bowl.
- add 2 tbsp coriander, few curry leaves, 1 inch ginger, grated, 1 tsp cumin and water.
- Mix well preparing a watery consistency batter.
- When the pan is super hot, pour the batter on the pan.
- Spread 1 tsp oil and allow to roast for 2 minutes or until the dosa turns crisp.
- Finally, enjoy the crispy Tomato Dosa Recipe with chutney.
- Firstly, in a mixer jar take 2 tomato, 1 cup rice flour, ½ cup rava, ½ tsp chilli powder and 1 tsp salt.
- Grind to smooth batter adding 2 cup water.
- Transfer the batter to a large bowl.
- Add 2 tbsp coriander, few curry leaves, 1 inch ginger, grated, 1 tsp cumin and water.
- Mix well preparing a watery consistency batter.
- When the pan is super hot, pour the batter on the pan.
- Spread 1 tsp oil and allow to roast for 2 minutes or until the dosa turns crisp.
- Finally, enjoy the crispy Tomato Dosa Recipe with chutney.
- Firstly, adjust the consistency of the batter by adding water as required. If the batter is thick, then the dosa will be soft.
- Also, adding red chilli powder brings a bright red colour to the dosa along with the spice.
- Additionally, you can also add maida for the batter to help in binding.
- Finally, the crispy Tomato Dosa Recipe tastes great when served hot with spicy chutney.
